Our Mission

Located 11 kilometres from the centre of Lusaka,
our health and wellness centre is surrounded by
botanical gardens keeping us from the hustle
and bustle of town. Our surroundings will put
you at ease straightaway.
The Health and Wellness Centre is dedicated
to offering a range of medical services embodying
both physical and mental health through a
multi-disciplinary team.
We are putting together a range of services that
compliment each other. We have an on -site gym
and swimming
pool to compliment our medical services, along
with short and long stay apartments to facilitate
comfortable rehabilitation should a patient need it.
